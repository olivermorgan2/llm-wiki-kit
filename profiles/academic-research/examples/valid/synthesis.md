# State of Sequence Modeling

## Scope

Sequence models from 2015 to 2026.

## Findings

Attention-based models dominate the benchmarks.

## Agreement

Most sources agree that scale improves quality.

## Disagreement

The efficiency trade-offs of attention remain contested.

## Evidence gaps

Long-context generalization is understudied.
